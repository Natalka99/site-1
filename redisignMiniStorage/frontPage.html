MCM Mini Storage facility in Macclenny FL was built keeping in mind that we wanted to provide a superior storing experience for our customers. We realize that moving or storing stuff is not really always a fun task. So we thought by providing the best of the best in mini self storage options, we could make a positive difference for you.

We want to be bigger. We have started with 20,000 square feet in storage unit space. This is just phase one. We have another 30,000 square feet planned at our storage facility in Macclenny Florida. We currently have 103 Non Climate Controller units and 39 Climate Controlled units.

We want to be better. Our indoor Climate Controlled safe storage units will be kept within a specific temperature range to minimize extreme heat and cold temperatures, protecting your belongings. In our Non-Climate Controlled facility units, we added a moisture barrier in the ceiling of every unit. This means that even if the building does experience extreme temperature, any «sweating» will be prevented from dripping onto your stored items.

We want you to have maximum acces. You will be able to access your stored belongings 24 hours a day, 7 days a week via a date with key code entry (coming soon). Each customer wil have their own code for added security.

We want a more secure mini self storage facility. Let’s face it, self slorage facilities can be a target for theft. Here at MCM Mini Storage in Macclenny Florida we are using a state of the art camera system to capture clear images from 200 feet away. Our mini storage facility will be monitored by cameras from every angle 24/7. Additionally, lighting will be in place 24/7 to help you see as you store or access your items. Your safety and security and the secutity of your stored belongings is important to us at MCM Mini Storage.

We also provide you with Renter’s Insurance options as you sign up for self storage space. This added service protects you if something were to happen here at our mini storage facility in Macclenny Florida.
